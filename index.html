Star Wars Battle

Welcome to Star Wars Battle! To Play the game, scroll through the list of characters at the top. When you have found characters that you like, click on the player panes below and they will appear there. Next, select a weapon for each player from the options below.

Now You're ready to play! Keep clicking the attack button to alternate attacks between player one and player two. When a players hitpoints has reached zero, a win message will appear at the bottom of the page and you will be given the option to play again! Fun!
